Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to offer white water rafting excursuons that are safe and enjoyable for the family or individuals.

Audience

This site is for white water rafting enthusiasts.White water rafting can be enjoyed by anyone who has a sense of adventure. Recommended ages for this activity is 5 years of age for beginning level terrains ,12 years old for mid level rafting and 14 years old and above for more advanced courses. It offers camping by the river, rafting and just enjoying the environment. It is a great way to get away from it all and enjoy the beauty of nature and the outdoors. This website will provide the user with information on various locations, packages, costs, and difficulty of the terrain. There is something for everyone to enjoy.
