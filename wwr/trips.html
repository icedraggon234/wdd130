Scenic Float

Scenic Float Trip, Level 1. Relaxing float to soak up the beauty and scenery. The gently moving waters flow across varying vistas creating an emjoyable nature show. There will be pauses where one can dip in the waters and splash around. Suitable for all ages, 5 and up.

Splash Adventure

Class II Rapids. This trip takes it up a notch with fun waves and a bit of the thrill factor. Qualified instructors will guide you in the technical skills needed to row through these mid level rapids. Class III includes some slower scenic float areas to take a break and gear up for the next exciting segment of the trip.

Rip Roaring

These rapids are at a more intense pace for the experienced thrill seeker. The course will include obstacles such as verticle drops, strong currents, big waves, and boulders to maneuver around. Safety precautions are taken but flips and ejections can occur! An expert guide will be on board to hep navigate the turns, spins, and nonstop excitement.

Adventure Duration Difficulty
Scenic Float 7km, 1.5-2 hours on the river Level 1, ages 5+
Splash Adventure 15km, 4-5 hours on the river Mid-Level, ages 12+
Rip Roaring 15km, 4-5 hours on the river Advanced, for experienced rafters
